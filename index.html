About Me

Hi, I'm Maraj — an iOS developer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh with a passion for building beautiful, intuitive mobile experiences. I work primarily in Swift and SwiftUI, following Apple's Human Interface Guidelines to create apps that feel right at home on iPhone and iPad.

I enjoy working across the full iOS stack: designing clean architectures with MVVM, integrating REST APIs with async/await, and sweating the details that make an app feel polished. I'm always exploring what's new from WWDC and looking for opportunities to ship something people love using.
